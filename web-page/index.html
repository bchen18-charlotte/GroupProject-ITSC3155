Our Mission Statement

We strive to create a fun working environment for our team so that we can invent and innovate various systems to make the world an easier place. A lot of our personal goals include making honest systems for others to use in a safe and secure way. We want to make sure that our team is always learning and growing together, and that we are always pushing ourselves to be better. Our good work ethic will allow us to create great products and have fun doing it!

Here are our rules:

  • Respect all team members
  • Communicate openly, honestly, and consistently
  • Support each other's growth and development. Offer help if you can
  • Respond to feedback in a timely manner
